## How to reach us

The group is run by volunteers. Please be patient — we may not be able to respond immediately.

**General enquiries:** [contact@ipace-owners.org](mailto:contact@ipace-owners.org)

**Media enquiries:** Please use the same address and mark your subject line with "Media:".

## Before you contact us

Please check the [FAQ](/faq/) — many common questions are answered there.

If you want to join the group, use the [Join page](/join/).

If you want to submit vehicle data, use the [Submit Vehicle Data](/submit-vehicle-data/) form.

## Want to help?

We are a volunteer-run initiative. If you have skills in any of the following areas and would
like to help, please get in touch:

- Legal or consumer rights
- Technical / battery expertise
- Data analysis
- Media / public relations
- Web or administration
- Dealer or automotive industry contacts

You can also indicate your interest when [joining the group](/join/).
