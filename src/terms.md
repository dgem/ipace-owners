## Nature of the group

The I-PACE Owners' Advocacy Group is an **informal owners' advocacy initiative**. It is not
currently incorporated as a company, charity, or formal legal entity. It does not have formal
legal representation unless and until that changes and members are informed.

## No legal advice

This website and this group do not provide legal advice. Nothing on this site should be taken
as legal advice. If you require legal advice about your specific situation — warranty claims,
consumer rights, or any other matter — you should consult a qualified solicitor or consumer
rights adviser.

## No client relationship

Joining this group or submitting data does not create a solicitor–client relationship or any
other professional services relationship with any individual or organisation.

## Accurate submissions

When submitting vehicle data or other information, owners are asked to provide information
that is accurate and complete to the best of their knowledge. We understand that not all
details may be known exactly — the forms are designed to accommodate uncertainty — but owners
should not knowingly submit false or misleading information.

## Use of submitted data

Data submitted by members may be used in anonymised aggregate reporting, subject to the
consent options you select during the submission process. Data may be shared with JLR in
the context of evidence-based engagement, in anonymised and aggregated form only, where
members have consented to this use.

## No guarantees

The group makes no guarantees about outcomes — warranty resolutions, recall outcomes, goodwill
payments, or any other matter. We organise and advocate; we do not control JLR's decisions.

## Changes to this statement

This statement may be updated as the group develops and its structure evolves. Members will
be informed of material changes.
