We are pleased to announce that the I-PACE Owners' Advocacy Group website is now live.

The group exists to bring together Jaguar I-PACE owners affected by traction battery faults,
recall concerns, battery degradation, and warranty uncertainty. We aim to collect structured,
verifiable evidence and engage constructively with JLR for fair outcomes.

## What's available now

- Public information pages explaining the group's purpose and approach
- Evidence methodology documentation
- A public evidence dashboard (placeholder — data collection coming soon)
- FAQ covering common questions about the group

## Coming soon

- Member registration and the join form
- Vehicle data submission form
- Member dashboard
- Backend data collection via Netlify Functions

[Join the group](/join/) or [read the FAQ](/faq/) to find out more.
