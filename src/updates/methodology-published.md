We have published the full [evidence methodology](/methodology/) for the I-PACE Owners'
Advocacy Group data collection.

The methodology explains:

- How data is collected through structured owner submissions
- The five verification levels applied to each entry
- What is published (anonymised aggregate statistics only)
- What is never published (personal data, full VINs)
- Known limitations of the dataset

Transparency about our methodology is important. We want owners, JLR, consumer bodies, and
the media to be able to understand and scrutinise our evidence base.
