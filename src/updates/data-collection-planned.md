We are preparing the vehicle data submission form for launch. The form covers:

- Vehicle details and battery State of Health
- HV battery work history (type, dates, duration)
- Recall status (H570, H571, H572)
- Loan car provision and experiences
- Payments, goodwill, and expense records
- Consent options for data use

Data will be collected, verified, and presented as anonymised aggregate statistics on the
public evidence dashboard.

[Read our methodology](/methodology/) for full details of how data will be collected and verified.
